Urban Sprawl in the US and Increasing Air Pollution

The purpose of this is to see how sprawl affects cities' air pollution. U.S. EPA data contains metrics for various pollutants from 1990 for hundreds of cities year-by-year and Census data is available for 1990, 2000, and 2010 including cities'
population density and for 2000 and 2010 additional factors like land area and housing units. A minor problem would be the number of different pollutants used and metrics but the sheer amount of cities makes up for it.
